What is Bonsai?

Bonsai is the art of growing trees and shrubs in containers, skillfully pruning and shaping them to create stunning miniature replicas of nature.

A Quick History

Bonsai originated in China over a thousand years ago and was later embraced and refined by the Japanese, becoming an art form that embodies harmony, balance, and the beauty of imperfection.

"The bonsai is not you working on the tree; you have to have the tree work on you."
-John Y. Naka
